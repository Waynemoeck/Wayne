This is the scratch game that we created. To shoot the arrow, press the right arrow key. Every time you hit the apple you get 5 points. When you hit the apple you will move on to the next level. Each level has a different background and a different difficulty. The speed of the apple moving up and down increases after each level is passed. After the game is completed, the background swtiches to the night city background and the screen says Game Over. The game will restart when the green flag is clicked again.

This is what the home screen of our multiple choice quiz looks like on the android device. On the top of the page there is a label that says start quiz. In the middle there is a label that says multiple choice quiz. There is also a button at the bottom of the page that says Start Quiz. When the user clicks that button they will begin the quiz and will be taken to the first question of the quiz.These are two of the question screens in the multiple choice quiz. The first is the screen of the first question of the quiz. There is a label at the top of the screen that says the question number. Then, there is a label that says the question. Next, thre are four buttons that are the four possible answers. At the bottom there is a label that says to choose an answer. When the correct answer is chosen the bottom label will become green and say correct. If the wrong answer is chosen, then the label will become red and say wrong. Also, when an answer is chosen, it will bring you to the nect question screen. Each question on the quiz works the same way as this. The second screen is the screen of the final question in the quiz. There is the label for the question number, the question, the choose an answer label, and the four buttons for the answers. The answer buttons work in the same way as all the other questions. In addition to the other buttons and labels, there is a button at the bottom of the screen that says Finish the Quiz. When this button is clicked it will bring you to the last screen of the quiz.
